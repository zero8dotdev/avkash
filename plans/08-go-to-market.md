# 08 — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Target: Indian SMEs in Tier 2+ Cities, Non-IT Sector

---

## The Core Problem We Solve (in their language)

Don't say "HRMS" or "SaaS". Say:

> "Apke employees ka leave WhatsApp pe approve karo. Salary slip bhi WhatsApp pe bhejo.
> PF aur ESI automatic calculate ho. Sab ek jagah."

Translation:

> "Approve leaves on WhatsApp. Send payslips on WhatsApp. PF and ESI calculated automatically. Everything in one place."

This is what they understand. This is what they'll buy.

---

## Phase 1: Get First 50 Paying Customers (Month 1–4)

### Strategy: Direct Founder Sales

**Do this yourself before building a sales team.**

1. **Map 3 target cities** (start small, go deep) — recommend: Indore, Surat, Jaipur
   - All have strong manufacturing/textile/trading clusters
   - Strong CA networks
   - Mid-size non-IT companies in abundance

2. **Find 10 CAs in each city**
   - LinkedIn + Justdial search: "CA firm [city]"
   - Ask for referral from your own CA
   - Visit local CII / FICCI chapter events (they happen monthly)

3. **CA pitch (15 minutes, one meeting):**
   - "I know you spend 20 hours/month on payroll for 5 clients. I can cut that to 2 hours."
   - Demo: payroll run for 50 employees → done in 5 minutes
   - Offer: 20% recurring commission. They use it free with their clients.
   - Leave behind: printed 1-pager in Hindi

4. **Ask each CA for 2–3 introductions to their clients**
   - You + CA together visit the business owner (this is how trust is built in tier 2)
   - Demo on-site: their phone, their WhatsApp, their employee names

5. **Onboard with high-touch support**
   - Import their employees via CSV (do it for them)
   - Set up WhatsApp bot for their company
   - First payroll run: you sit with them on a video call
   - They will tell their friends

## Phase 2: Build Repeatable Machine (Month 4–10)

### Channel 1: CA Reseller Network (Primary)

**Goal: 100 active CA resellers by Month 10**

- Dedicated CA portal: manage all their client orgs in one dashboard
- Monthly CA newsletter: compliance changes, new features, tips
- WhatsApp group for CA partners (direct line to your team)
- Quarterly webinar: new features + compliance updates (CAs love CPE hours)
- Case study: "How CA Sharma in Indore automated payroll for 8 clients and added ₹40,000/month recurring income"

**Outreach:**

- ICAI regional chapters have email lists
- LinkedIn: search "CA [city]" + connect with note
- Referral: each CA who joins refers 2 more → exponential

### Channel 2: WhatsApp Referral Program

**For existing customers (business owners):**

- "Refer a business owner friend. When they pay their first month, you get 1 free month."
- Owner shares a referral link via WhatsApp to their own business network
- Business owner peer groups (Rotary, Young Entrepreneurs Forum, Lions Club) are powerful

**Mechanics:**

- Referral link tied to org
- Auto-tracked in billing system
- Credit applied automatically

### Channel 3: Content in Hindi (Slow Burn, High Value)

**YouTube (most effective for tier 2 India):**

- Channel name: "Smart HR India" or "HR सरल बनाओ"
- Videos (5–10 min each):
  - "PF kaise calculate kare? Simple formula"
  - "Leave management Excel se kaise kare better"
  - "Payslip kaise banaye apne employee ka"
  - "ESI kya hai? Kab applicable hota hai?"
  - "Form 16 kya hai aur kaise generate kare"
- Each video ends with: "Yeh sab automatically ho sakta hai Avkash mein. Free mein shuru karo."

**Google Search (Hindi + English mix):**

- Target keywords: "PF calculation software India", "HR software for small business India", "leave management app India"
- Blog posts: solve one specific problem completely, end with Avkash CTA

**Start content by Month 2.** These take 6–9 months to pay off but compound forever.

### Channel 4: Industry Association Partnerships

**Target associations:**

- Surat Textile Traders Association
- Rajkot Engineering Industries Association
- Nashik Industries & Manufacturers Association
- CII SME chapters in 5 cities

**Pitch:**

- Offer 3-month free trial for all association members
- Co-branded announcement via their newsletter/WhatsApp group
- Speak at one of their events (5-minute demo)
- Association gets: free account to manage member data (optional)

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 10+)

### Inside Sales Team

- 2 SDRs (Sales Development Reps) + 1 Account Executive
- Inbound: demo requests from website, CA referrals
- Outbound: targeted outreach to businesses in industrial clusters
- SDR profile: Hindi-speaking, familiar with GST/PF/ESI, based in target city
- Tools: Avkash CRM (use our own product), WhatsApp Business

### Enterprise Sales

- Hire 1 enterprise AE targeting 200–1,000 employee companies
- These deals: ₹50,000–2,00,000/year, 3–6 month sales cycle
- Requires: security questionnaire response, data processing agreement, SLA
- Self-hosted option is the closer for these deals

### Performance Marketing (After Product-Market Fit)

Don't spend on ads until:

- You have 10 customers who love the product
- You know which industries convert best
- You have a 30-day payback period on CAC

Then:

- Google Search ads: high intent, expensive but targeted ("payroll software India")
- Meta ads: target by job title (Business Owner, HR Manager) + city + industry
- Budget: ₹50,000/month initially, scale based on CAC data

## Pricing in Sales Conversation

**Don't lead with price. Lead with pain:**

1. "How long does your payroll take today?" (Answer: 2–3 days)
2. "And what happens if PF is filed late?" (Answer: fine + interest)
3. "With Avkash, payroll runs in 20 minutes. PF file is auto-generated."
4. _Then_ mention price: "₹60 per employee per month. For a 60-person company, that's ₹3,600/month."
5. Frame: "Less than what you'd pay a part-time accountant for 2 hours."
6. Close: "Let me help you set it up right now. It takes 30 minutes."

---

## Customer Success / Retention

**Churn is the enemy. At ₹5,000/month ARR per company, losing a customer costs 12 months to recover.**

- Month 1: White-glove onboarding (call, CSV import, first payroll run)
- Month 2: Check-in: "Did payslips go out on time? Any issues?"
- Month 3: Share their first payroll report (they love seeing data)
- Month 6: "You have 65 employees now — want to set up the performance review module?"
- Ongoing: Monthly WhatsApp message (not email) with 1 tip + product update
- Annual: Renewal conversation 60 days before — offer annual discount

**NPS survey at Month 3.** Anyone who rates 9–10: ask for a video testimonial or LinkedIn post.

---

## Launch Events

**City-by-city launch (not a big national launch):**

1. Partner with local CA association for a breakfast event
2. Invite 20 business owners (CA brings them)
3. 30-min product demo live, Q&A
4. Everyone gets 3-month free trial + setup help
5. 2 of 20 convert in month 1 → word spreads in that city's business community

Do this in 3 cities in Month 3–4. Repeat quarterly in new cities.
